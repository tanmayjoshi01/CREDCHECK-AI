How CredCheck AI Works

Our multi-layered verification system combines advanced natural language processing, computer vision, and real-time fact-checking.

psychology_alt

1. Linguistic Analysis

We analyze the text for sensationalism, emotional manipulation, and bias patterns commonly found in fake news. Our AI detects clickbait structures and subjective language.

image_search

2. Image-Text Consistency

If an image is provided, we compare its content with the text. We detect if the image is unrelated, manipulated, or taken out of context to mislead the reader.

fact_check

3. Source Verification

We cross-reference the claims with a vast database of trusted news outlets. We check if major sources are reporting the same story and verify specific facts.

score

4. Trust Score Calculation

Finally, we aggregate all data points into a single Trust Score (0-100). This score represents the overall credibility probability, giving you a quick and easy verdict.
